CP Cost Estimator

Scope and size Confluent Platform clusters, configure connectors, and estimate licensing costs across production, non-production, and development environments.

How to use this estimator
  1. Set up clusters — Choose the number of clusters, give each a name, and select the environment type (Production, Non-Production, or Dev).
  2. Size each cluster — For every cluster, enter the number of nodes and billed nodes for each CP component (Kafka Broker, ZooKeeper, Connect, etc.). Toggle components on or off as needed.
  3. Configure connectors — Enter the number of Premium and Non-Premium connectors and the CP Connector Pack count in the Connectors section.
  4. Enter pricing — Expand the Pricing section and fill in the per-node and per-connector rates for Production and Non-Production environments.
  5. Review totals — The Billed Nodes Summary and cost totals update automatically as you make changes.
  6. Export & share — Use Download Excel to generate a shareable workbook with all configurations and pricing, or Download PDF for a print-ready summary.
  7. Re-import — Use Upload Excel to load a previously exported workbook and continue editing.

Your inputs are saved automatically in the browser. Use Cmd + Shift + R to hard-refresh if the page feels stale.

Pricing & Licensing Rules
1: Disaster Recovery (DR) nodes are priced at 50% of the Production price
2: UAT, Staging, Perf is considered as Non-Prod
3: Dev is free
4: Connect workers and Connectors to be sold in a Pack

Tip: Hard refresh on Mac with Cmd + Shift + R.
